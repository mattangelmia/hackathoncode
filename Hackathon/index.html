Stanley Hudson

Stanley is a nationally-recognized pretzel artisan based in Scranton, PA who loves to leverage his personal skills in sales to provide the best experience he can offer with his pretzels. With award winning food trucks and a one of a kind pop up installations Stanley has found a way to bring his love for pretzels to places unimaginable.

Supreme x Stanley’s Pretzels

Our best-selling collaboration with iconic skateboard brand, Supreme New York. We offered a one of a kind pop up shop in the historical flagship located in the heart of Soho. 10% of proceeds was donated to Boys and Girls club of NYC.

Purple Rain

To celebrate the life of legendary musician Prince, we created the limited edition Purple Rain pretzels. All proceeds from this collaboration go to Prince’s organization, Love 4 One Another, which focuses on driving better outcomes for humanity.

Nike Pretzel Workshop

For a COVID safe event we partnered with nike to provide a unique customization workshop where we allowed customers to customize their own pretzel at the NIKE headquarters.

Boys and Girls Club

On a hot summer day in July we surprised the Children at the boys and girls club with a Inaugural event called “Pretzels and Popsicles” Where we hosted a day at the club to offer Children snacks after their sporting events
